    R.I.P

    Ladies and gentlemen
    Pick a side ——

    triangle or rectangle
    hair bow or smoking pipe
    high heels or Oxford shoes
    red or blue
    Cleopatra or Tutankhamun

    Pick a side, now
    lady or gentleman
    and may you rest in peace
    in your assigned
    Restroom™️
