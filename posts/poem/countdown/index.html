    Countdown

    Countdown

    November 19, 2022
    poem, english

    We can count to ten with one hand

    Tie a noose, Ten
    Hang a hook, Nine
    Fire a shot, Eight
    Pinch your nose, Seven
    Make the call, Six
    Slap a face, Five

    Shhhhh….

    Three at the table
    Two chopsticks in hand
    Pointed finger
    then clenched fist

    Count to 10
    in binary
    starting from Abandon
    through Martyrdom and Menopause
    ending at Zenith

    And start over again
