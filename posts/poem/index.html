      Padded cell

      June 26, 2023
      english

      There (she) is beneath the soft ceiling Nails too short to tear down scraps of yellow wallpaper Mask too thin to fend off smoke from the attic aflame Limbs too weak to hold up an emaciated body upside down Lips too stiff to scream in the voice of the mother There (she) is amid the solid silence too sane to dissolve into the letters left with no escape but to squeeze in between the lines

      Vampire

      January 2, 2023
      english

      The night I turned into a vampire I looked into the mirror and saw the reflection of nothing but void My existence shattered into pieces flinging themselves out of reality into the otherly to be scattered across the words of others No one is paying me a visit tonight as I lie in my bed, or casket, awaiting the break of dawn

      今天的回答

      November 28, 2022
      poem, 白纸

      你问我，皇帝的新装有多漂亮 我的喉咙已被钳住 只有铁链叮当作响 你问我，皇帝的新装有多漂亮 我取出口中的棉签 祈祷不要变成待宰的羔羊 你问我，皇帝的新装有多漂亮 我只能纵身一跳 让重力替我传出回响 你问我，皇帝的新装有多漂亮 却又将我的声音囚禁 勒令我静默着等待释放 你问我，皇帝的新装有多漂亮 火光和浓烟已模糊了我的双眼 但终会有人告诉你答案 就在那条不存在的路上 你问我，皇帝的新装有多漂亮 我不要说谎 于是和TA一起 举起白纸一张
