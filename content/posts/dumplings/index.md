Surely I mustn’t be the only one

to find a resemblance<br>
between floating dumplings<br>
and bloated cadavers<br> 

soaked<br> 
over nine months<br> 
simmered<br>
nearly three decades

skin wearing thin<br>
insides spilling out<br> 
still raw<br>
and frozen

Surely I mustn’t be the only one
